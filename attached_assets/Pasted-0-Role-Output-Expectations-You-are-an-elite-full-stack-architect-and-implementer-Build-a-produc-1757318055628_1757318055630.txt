1) Business Context & Actors

Entities and roles (each with login & permissions):

Super Admin (platform owner): global settings, tenants, pricing templates, global reports.

OEM Admin (e.g., Tata, Kia): manages its dealerships, brand vehicles, visibility on metrics.

Dealership Admin: manages its showrooms, assigns Sales Persons, sets per-partner pricing overrides.

Showroom Manager: raises Work Orders, verifies & approves completed jobs, sets Sales Person commissions (fixed or %).

Sales Person (under Showroom): visible only to its showroom; earns commission per installation order.

Detailer Studio (partner): a studio business that can be allocated to one or more showrooms/dealerships.

Installer (partner): an individual/freelancer who can be allocated similarly; can be linked to a studio or separate.

Partner Admin/Staff (for Detailer/Installer): sees assigned jobs, acknowledges, schedules, starts, completes, uploads proof.

Multi-allocation rules:

A showroom can allocate multiple detailers/installers.

Allocation can be done at dealership level (applies to all its showrooms) or per showroom.

Pricing is configurable per partner × showroom/dealership × vehicle × service.

Sales Person commission is configurable per showroom (amount or %).

2) Core Definitions & Status Machines
2.1 Work Order (WO)

Raised only by Showroom (not OEM or Dealership).

Purpose: request a PPF installation on a specific vehicle/service bundle.

WO Statuses:

DRAFT → SUBMITTED → ASSIGNED → (reflected from Job Card states) → COMPLETED_PENDING_APPROVAL → APPROVED → CLOSED

Cancellation: CANCELLED (if WO withdrawn before approval).

Rework path: REWORK_REQUESTED → back to job in progress → returns to COMPLETED_PENDING_APPROVAL.

Assignment:

Auto-assign to the highest-priority allocated partner (see allocation table) OR manual selection by Showroom Manager.

On assignment, create Job Card.

2.2 Job Card (JC) — owned by Partner (Detailer/Installer)

Mirrors execution; linked 1:1 to a WO.

JC Statuses:

AWAITING_ACK (partner must acknowledge) → ACKNOWLEDGED → SCHEDULED (visit_date/time) → IN_PROGRESS → COMPLETED (proof uploaded) → PENDING_APPROVAL (awaiting showroom approval) → APPROVED (locks pricing & commission) → CLOSED

Failure paths:

NO_SHOW / CANCELLED_BY_CUSTOMER / PARTS_PENDING (optional) → may transition to RESCHEDULED.

REWORK_REQUESTED (adds rework child JC or re-opens to IN_PROGRESS).

Proof Requirements:

Minimum N images (configurable), remarks, optional video, and checklist (quality gates).

Approval:

Only Showroom Manager (or configured approver) can approve.

On approval, trigger payouts computation & commission calculation.

4) RBAC Matrix (high-level)

Super Admin: everything.

OEM Admin: CRUD dealerships/showrooms under OEM; view all metrics for OEM; cannot raise WOs.

Dealership Admin: CRUD showrooms under dealership, manage allocations (dealership scope), pricing overrides at dealership scope; view dealership metrics.

Showroom Manager: raise & manage WOs, approve Job Cards, manage showroom allocations and pricing overrides at showroom scope, set Sales Person commissions, view showroom metrics.

Sales Person: view their orders/earnings only (no approvals).

Partner Admin/Staff: view assigned WOs/JCs, acknowledge/schedule/start/complete, upload proofs; no access to showroom private data.

Enforce strict tenant isolation (OEM boundary). All queries scoped to OEM unless Super Admin.

5) Business Rules
5.1 Allocation & Assignment

On WO SUBMITTED, system selects partner:

If a manual partner is chosen by Showroom Manager, honor that.

Else pick highest-priority active allocation at showroom level.

Else fallback to dealership level allocations.

Create JC with AWAITING_ACK. Notify partner.

5.2 Job Flow & Approval

Partner must move states in order: AWAITING_ACK → ACKNOWLEDGED → set SCHEDULED (with datetime) → IN_PROGRESS → upload proofs → COMPLETED → PENDING_APPROVAL.

Showroom can APPROVE, REJECT (with reason and optional rework), or REQUEST_REWORK.

On APPROVED, freeze pricing snapshot and commission snapshot and compute payouts.

5.3 Pricing

Resolve price in this order:

Exact match rule: (partner, showroom, vehicle_model, service)

Fallback: (partner, showroom, service)

Fallback: (partner, dealership, vehicle_model, service)

Fallback: (partner, dealership, service)

Else error: “No pricing rule found”.

Support future-dated rules (effective_from/to).

5.4 Commission

Resolve commission rule in this order:

Exact match: (showroom, sales_person, service)

Fallback: (showroom, sales_person)

Fallback: (showroom, service)

Fallback: (showroom default)

Else: 0

Compute amount = min(max(basis_value, floor), cap) where basis is % of (approved price) or flat amount.

5.5 SLAs & Notifications

SLA: AWAITING_ACK > X hours → alert showroom + partner supervisor.

SLA: SCHEDULED but not IN_PROGRESS by schedule time → alert.

Events send notifications (email/SMS/WhatsApp/push) via queue.

7) Validation & Edge Cases

Soft delete for most masters; preserve referential integrity.
