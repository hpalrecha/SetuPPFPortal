Task

There are two limitations in the current setup:

Partner → Showroom Allocation

Today: Partner (Installer/Detailer) can be linked to only one showroom (one-to-one).

Real-world: A single partner may serve multiple showrooms within or across dealerships.

Pricing Rules

Today: Payout rules for detailers/installers require partner type + service category + vehicle model + amount + effective date.

Real-world: Vehicle model dependency is unnecessary. Rules should apply per service category across all vehicles.

We need to redesign allocation and simplify pricing to match actual business needs.

Action
1. Partner → Showroom (One-to-Many Mapping)

DB Update:

Remove partner.showroom_id.

Create new partner_showroom_mapping table:

partner_id

showroom_id

status (active/inactive)

created_at, updated_at

UI/UX:

In Partner creation/update → Showroom field becomes multi-select.

Validation: At least one showroom must be selected.

Workflow:

Job Card creation → only partners mapped to that showroom appear.

Reporting, payouts, commissions → filter by partner_id + showroom_id.

2. Simplified Pricing Rule (Remove Vehicle Model)

DB Update:

Remove vehicle_model field from pricing_rule.

New schema:

partner_type (Detailer/Installer)

service_category_id

payout_amount

effective_date

UI/UX:

In Pricing Rule form → remove Vehicle Model field.

Service Category + Partner Type + Amount → mandatory.

Workflow:

During payout → match rule on partner_type + service_category.

Apply payout regardless of vehicle model.

Legacy rules → migrate by consolidating multiple vehicle-model entries into one service-category-level rule.

Check

Partner Allocation Example:

Rajesh Detailers linked to Showroom A (Hyundai) + Showroom B (Kia).

When a job card is raised in Showroom A → Rajesh available only if mapped.

Pricing Rule Example:

Old: Detailer + “Ceramic Coating” + “Hyundai Creta” → ₹2000.

New: Detailer + “Ceramic Coating” → ₹2000 (applies to all vehicles).

Outcome

Partners can flexibly serve multiple showrooms without duplicate accounts.

Pricing rules are much simpler, easier to manage, and universal per service category.

OEM/Dealer data integrity remains intact:

Partner visibility limited to mapped showrooms.

Payouts/commissions apply consistently and fairly across vehicles.
